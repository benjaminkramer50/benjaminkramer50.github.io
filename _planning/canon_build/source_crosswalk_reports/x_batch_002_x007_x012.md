## Source Policy Notes

- Edition series, reference corpora, catalog records, and text-access metadata support identity, alias, edition, provenance, or boundary context.
- They do not by themselves prove canon inclusion.
- Anthology rows must distinguish complete works, substantial selections, excerpts, individual poems/stories, author headings, and context material.
- Bloom review batches are internal triage, not external source evidence.
