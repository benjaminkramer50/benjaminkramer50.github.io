## Main Blockers

- Corpus/database rows are not canon votes. Perseus, PHI, SagaDB, ARLIMA, PhiloBiblon, Dante projects, and OWC need lower evidence weights than anthology/course-list layers.
- Many anthology rows are excerpts or selections, not complete-work inclusions.
- Author headings, introductions, timelines, maps, bibliographies, glossaries, permissions, illustrations, and companion-site context rows must not become work rows.
- Title collisions require creator-aware matching: `Metamorphoses`, `Invisible Man`, `Passing`, `The Street`, `The New Negro`, `Histories`, `Odes`, and `Poems`.
- Cycle and contained-work policy is required for `Oresteia`, Arthurian Vulgate material, `Cane`, `The Canterbury Tales`, `Piers Plowman`, slave narratives, and poem-level anthology entries.
- Columbia/Princeton philosophy, scripture, film, music, and art rows require boundary policy before scoring.
